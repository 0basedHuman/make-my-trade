# Weekly Review Protocol

## Purpose
Summarize one week of autonomous paper-trading results without mutating strategy automatically.

## Required outputs
- total confirmed trades
- total paper positions opened
- open vs closed positions
- win/loss count
- average hold time
- false positives
- false negatives
- setup family breakdown
- market regime notes
- chain quality failure summary
- proposed strategy changes

## Constraints
- propose changes only
- do not auto-edit strategy_rules.yaml
- do not auto-fine-tune models
- keep proposals explicit and reversible
